ABOUT US

Nowadays with the increase in overseas education, the process of moving to a new country could be a hectic one, our application plays a part to help students & people from other fields to find their ideal roommate.
Our project uses the review-based model, to work through the process of finding an ideal roommate. Our project is a social platform that lets the user see what kind of a roommate he/she wants based on the reviews provided by the “Target roommates” previous roommate,
Once they have found their ideal roommate, they can contact them through various media.
The review could be made on a certain standard set of factors, such as cleanliness, punctuality, compatibility, etc., which could be only made by a verified user.
